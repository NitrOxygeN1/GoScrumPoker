For privacy requests or to revoke Google access to the app, see the Privacy Policy and your Google Account permissions.

Google OAuth verification

App operators submitting this product for Google OAuth verification should ensure the deployed URLs match the OAuth consent screen, including links to this Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Service status

Hosted deployments expose a health check at /health. If the app does not load, verify that the server is running and that WebSockets are allowed through your network or proxy.
