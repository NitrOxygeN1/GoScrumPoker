Quick guide to planning poker with GoScrumPoker

Create a room

  1. Open the app home page.
  2. Enter your display name.
  3. Click Create room.
  4. Share the room link or room ID with your team.

Join a room

  1. Open the invite link, or paste the room ID on the home page.
  2. Enter your name and click Join room.

Vote on a story

  • Pick a card (Fibonacci-style values, ?, or ☕).
  • You can change your vote before the round is revealed.
  • Other participants see that you have voted, but not your value, until reveal.

Reveal and reset

  • Reveal votes — shows everyone's cards to the room.
  • Reset round — clears votes for the next story.

Google Meet

When embedded in Google Meet, use the side panel at full width. Sign in with Google uses a full-page redirect (not a popup). If login fails inside Meet, try opening the app in a browser tab and signing in there first.

Sign in with Google

Optional Google Sign-In may be enabled on your deployment. When available, use Sign in with Google from the app. Profile data use is described in the Privacy Policy.
